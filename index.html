At Straffon Orthodontics, each individual’s treatment plan is as unique as their fingerprint; each smile, a personal signature. Meet Dr. Straffon

DENVER’S LEADING EXPERT IN THE USE OF CUSTOM ORTHODONTICS

Dr. Derek Straffon attended Indiana University, and then went on to receive his Doctor of Dental Surgery and Master of Science in Orthodontics from the nationally ranked University of Michigan.

He is a recipient of the prestigious Thomas A. Graber Award for Research in Orthodontics, Top Orthodontist by 5280 Magazine, and was named Top Doctor among USA Top Dentists.

In his free time, you will find him enjoying the outdoors of Colorado running triathlons, mountain biking, skiing, and spending time with his family.
